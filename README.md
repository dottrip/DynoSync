# DynoSync

AI-powered car modification archive SaaS for hardcore car enthusiasts. Converts plain-language mod inputs into high-quality personalized performance dashboards — solving the pain of fragmented, hard-to-track tuning and dyno data, while delivering a "digital garage" with strong social sharing appeal.

## What It Does

- **Digital Garage** — manage multiple project cars, track dyno runs, WHP, torque, and 0-60 data
- **AI Mod Advisor** — plain-language input gets parsed into structured data; AI suggests next mods and compares options
- **Dyno Sheet Scanning** — scan paper dyno sheets with the camera to import historical data
- **Social Sharing** — generate 9:16 share cards optimized for Instagram Stories/Reels, with QR code linking to public build profile
- **Global Leaderboard** — compete by WHP within your car model

## Development Roadmap

**Phase 1 — Core Loop** (8-10 weeks)
Auth, vehicle management, manual dyno entry, basic dashboard. Validate that users will input their data.

**Phase 2 — Social & Growth Hooks** (6-8 weeks)
Public build profiles (Web, SEO-indexable), share card generation, leaderboard, before/after comparison. Validate that shared links drive organic signups.

**Phase 3 — AI Differentiation** (6-8 weeks)
Plain-language mod parsing, AI suggestions, mod comparison analysis, paper dyno sheet scanning. Validate AI accuracy and satisfaction.

**Phase 4 — Monetization** (4-6 weeks)
Stripe subscription (Pro tier), feature gates, payment flow. Target 3-5% Free → Pro conversion.

**Phase 5 — Elite & Growth Flywheel** (ongoing)
Elite tier, achievements, parts affiliate integration, multi-device sync.
